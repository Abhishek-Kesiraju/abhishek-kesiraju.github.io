The First Switch

Ending notes - 2025

After working for 3 years in a team, which lost its way over time. I feel at peace... Unsure how long it stays that way. But I am currently making sure that I take it slow and reset myself mentally & physically. I dont like using the "T" word for a workplace or a team, but when it starts to take a toll on you for close to 2 years, it is time for a change. It took me close to an year, to prepare for this internal lateral move. It is closely after the first 5 interviews, I started seeing the patterns and started realizing "Interviewing is its own muscle". It needs to be trained. It is a new domain, but in the same org. Some new rules, that I setup for myself after overworking into burnout for the last two years. "Time & Health are the real currency". Irony, the domain which actually moves currency, started moving this real currency away from me. But indeed, it paid me with experience, a beginner's comfort cushion, and OG mentors that I will forever remember and cherish. I will try my best to stay in touch with them.
