First Month at CIAM

December - 2025
When I worked as a Programmer Analyst in IAM, before post-grad, I did not quite like the concept and motive of Identity and Access management. I was hesitant that CIAM was just another name for the same vertical. But they are two entirely different domains that probably share few security-related ideologies like "CIA".

IAM - Identity and Access Management is focused on providing access to employee's of an org. Customer Identity and access Management deals with actual users of your software trying to make operations and you're verifying people are who they claim to be and they have the right kind of access to do so.
I had an understanding of how OAuth works using JWT & PingFed at a high level for APIs to communicate with one another. But there seems to be a world of security stuff, which makes Authentication and Trust a platform of its own. Next things to learn so I can contribute well in this space are OAuth, OIDC, and SAML. Being able to integrate APIs with Okta, Auth0 etc
